Exotic dance moves

Yes you are right! As part of my post-doctorate work, I did study exotic tribal dances. To answer your questions, my favourite dances are as follows, with definitions:

Polynesian chicken dance
A little known but very influential dance dating back as far as 300BC, a whole village would dance around in a circle like chickens, to encourage their livestock to be "fruitful".
Icelandic brownian shuffle
Before the re-introduction of roundabouts in Iceland in 1929, there was an odd tradition of drivers performing a variation of a left-turn (counter-clockwise) shuffle in order to indicate a right-turn (clockwise).
Arctic robot dance
Just like the robot dance you know and love, but with more ice and windchill.
